Let's understand the Stages of Mental Health

1. Optimal Mental Health(Flourishing)

The person feels resilient, motivated, and fulfilled. They are able to manage stress well, form meaningful relationships, and have a strong sense of purpose.

2. Good Mental Health (Stable)

The individual feels generally well and capable, although they might experience occasional stress or setbacks that they manage effectively.

3. Mild Mental Health Concerns (Struggling)

The person experiences occassional or mild mental health symptoms like stress, sadness, or anxiety that impact daily life but are manageable.

4. Moderate Mental Health Issues (Coping)

The person is dealing with more pronounced symptoms of mental health conditions (e.g., anxiety, depression) that impact daily functioning

5. Severe Mental Health Issues (Crisis)

The individual experiences severe, ongoing mental health symptoms that greatly interfere with daily life and can feel overwhelming or debilitating.
